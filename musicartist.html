Masayoshi Takanaka

Takanaka is a city pop & jazz fusion artist and is widely recognized for his role in garnering popularity for the genres. His Spotify features many of his most famous albums although it is still not fully up to date. He still does live concerts at 69 years old but they are unfortunately exclusively in Japan.

Some of My Personal Favorite Songs:

  • E.S.P
  • An Insatiable High
  • Blue Lagoon
  • Brasilian Skies
  • Taj Mahal
